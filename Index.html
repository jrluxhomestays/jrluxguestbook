Chapter Two · Our Guiding Principle

The JRLUX Philosophy

Every JRLUX home is built on a single conviction: hospitality is not a transaction, it is a preparation. Before you ever received your check-in details, this residence was walked through, checked, and set right — bedding pressed, surfaces cleared, every drawer and cupboard restored to order.

"We prepare the home as if family were arriving."

It is a simple standard, but a demanding one. It means nothing is left to chance, and nothing is assumed. It means the small, unglamorous details — a stocked pantry shelf, a spare fuse, a clearly labelled switch — are attended to long before you notice their absence. This is the quiet discipline behind every JRLUX residence.

Chapter Three · Our Shared Responsibility

Comfort Meets Contribution

JRLUX operates on a self-service model — not to ask more of you, but to invite you into something better: a home that runs on mutual trust rather than transactional service, and where the comfort you enjoy is, in part, a comfort you help sustain.

We call this our Pay-It-Forward concept — a simple idea, carried through three quiet acts.

01
Leave It Better
Every guest is invited to leave the residence a little better than they found it — a habit of care that costs nothing and means everything to the next arrival.
02
Pass the Comfort On
The essentials stocked for you — pantry staples, laundry supplies, toiletries — are shared resources. Use what you need, and where you can, leave enough for the guest who follows.
03
A Better Stay, Together
Mindful use of electricity, water, and shared facilities keeps this home sustainable and this experience consistent — for you, and for every guest after you.

Chapter Six · A Few Gentle Guidelines

House Guidelines

Not rules in the strict sense — simply the understandings that keep this home comfortable for you, and just as comfortable for the guest who arrives after you.

1
Self-Service Home — This is a self-catering residence. Daily housekeeping is not provided. All cleaning supplies, laundry detergent, and kitchen cleaning products are fully stocked for your use throughout your stay.
2
Maximum Guests — This residence comfortably hosts up to 2 guests. Unregistered visitors are not permitted to stay overnight, and guests not declared at booking are kindly asked to make prior arrangements with your host.
3
No Smoking — Strictly no smoking inside the residence or on the balcony, including e-cigarettes and vaping. This helps preserve the home for every guest who stays after you.
4
Quiet Hours — This is a residential building. No parties or gatherings beyond registered guests. Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM daily.
5
No Pets — Pets of any kind are not permitted inside the residence or building premises.
6
Care For The Residence — Please treat the home and its furnishings with the same care we prepared it with. Any damage beyond normal wear will be discussed directly and fairly. Report any issues to your host as soon as you notice them.
7
Access Cards & Keys — Please keep all access cards and keys safe throughout your stay, and return them upon checkout. Lost items will be charged at replacement cost.
8
Check-In / Check-Out — Check-in: 1:00 PM. Check-out: 1:00 PM. A full 24-hour stay, guaranteed. Early check-in and late checkout are subject to availability — please enquire with your host in advance.
9
Cleaning Fee & Policy — A modest cleaning fee applies per booking and is already included in your reservation. Guests who leave checkout tasks incomplete may be asked to cover a small additional charge.
10
Electricity & Utilities — Please switch off the air conditioning, lights, and appliances whenever you leave the residence. Your mindful use helps keep this home — and its rates — sustainable.

Chapter Ten · Before You Go

A Thoughtful Departure

Checkout is at 1:00 PM. Think of this less as a checklist, and more as a small closing ritual — the same care shown to you, passed forward to the next guest. All supplies are provided; there is nothing to purchase.

Launder any used towels — place in the washing machine, add the detergent provided, and run a full wash cycle.
Hang washed towels on the drying rack — spread them out to air dry before you leave.
Wash any used dishes, pots, and utensils — dish soap is provided. Leave them on the drying rack.
Dispose of all rubbish — place waste in the rubbish bags provided, then bring it to the refuse room on your floor. Please do not leave rubbish inside the residence.
Return all access cards and keys — place them in the designated tray near the entrance, or contact your host for return instructions.
Switch off the air conditioning — check the bedroom and living hall.
Switch off all lights — including the bathroom, kitchen, and any night lights.
Lock the front door — ensure it is fully locked behind you.
Send your host a short message — letting us know you've departed is always appreciated.
Thank you for completing these simple steps. Your care keeps this home running smoothly and ready for the next guest. Safe travels — and we hope to welcome you back soon.
